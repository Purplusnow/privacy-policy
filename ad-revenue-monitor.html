About This App

AdMob AdSense Dashboard is an Android app that lets you check your AdMob and AdSense revenue quickly without opening a browser. Sign in with your Google account and see your earnings dashboard instantly.

Data Usage

This app accesses the following Google APIs on your behalf:

  • AdMob API (admob.readonly, admob.report) — to read your AdMob earnings and reports
  • AdSense Management API (adsense.readonly) — to read your AdSense account data

All data is fetched directly from Google's APIs and displayed only on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your revenue data.
